Vexter App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

Vexter App is owned and operated by BUIKE TECHNOLOGIES. We value user privacy and are committed to protecting personal information.

Information We Collect

Vexter App may collect the following information from users:

  • Username
  • Email Address
  • Password
  • Wallet and transaction information
  • Device and app usage information
  • We do not sell user data to third parties.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide virtual number services
  • Process wallet funding and payments
  • Send password reset emails
  • Improve app security and performance
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized activities
  • Display ads using Google AdMob

Refund Policy

Users may request a refund if a verification code is not received within the supported waiting period. Refunds are processed back to the user's wallet balance after verification of the failed order.

Terms of Service

By using Vexter App, users agree to use the platform responsibly and only for lawful purposes. Any misuse, fraudulent activity, spam-related activity, or violation of supported platform policies may result in account suspension or termination.

Secure Usage

We continuously monitor platform activity to maintain a safe and secure environment for all users. Our system is designed to provide reliable virtual number services while helping prevent abuse and unauthorized activities.

Third-Party Services

Children's Privacy

Vexter App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Account Deletion

Users can request permanent deletion of their account and all associated data at any time. To request account deletion, please contact us via email:
